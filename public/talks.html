Profile

Daniel Bünzli has a Master of Science in Computer Science from the École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL). Versatile and flexible, his domains of expertise include:

  • Web, UNIX system, database, 2D & 3D graphics programming
  • Data processing and visualisation, analytical design
  • Human-computer interaction
  • Interactive installations, embedded systems with sensors, actuators, LEDs, etc.
  • Mobile platforms (Web, iOS, Android…)

He works in short evolving development cycles, guided by the critical feedback of his partners to understand their vision and be as close as possible to their real needs.

These assets make him the perfect candidate to work with creative partners, in small teams, on unique and demanding projects.

His interest for computer science research and his work in free (libre) software keep him up to date with the latest innovations.
