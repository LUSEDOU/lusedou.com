Bitsport 2020

Bitsports is a cross-platform app that serves as a marketplace for sports teams. With Bitsports, you can find sports teams in your area, join them, and participate in their events. You can also create your own sports team, invite your friends to join, and organize events. With Bitsports, you can connect with other sports enthusiasts, share your passion for sports, and have fun playing your favorite sports.
